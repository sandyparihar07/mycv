About Me

My name is Sandeep Parihar (Sandy) and I am a SEO, Digital Marketing and eCommerce Website Expert who brings your website in the real world. I have been working in the IT Service & Technology Industry since the year 2015, and have been loving every minute of it. I graduated from Gujarat University in 2013 with a Bachelor of Computer Application degree.


Initially, I had started my career with a SEO executive, Ahmedabad in 2015. It was my first step to becoming a Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Expert. Then, I joined one of the IT Firm and I am really thankful that I got the opportunity to prove myself.


There i got a chance to work on Ecommerce Projects and worked on WordPress, WooCommerce, Shopify, NetSuite, and Zoho CMS & Ecommerce platform. I showed my skills and gain experience of SEO, Web Design and Web Development, Based on my work i got an Ecommerce & Project Manager position. Currently I am working as a SEO & Digital Marketing Team Leader along with that i am an Ecommerce Project Manager.


I'm very passionate and dedicated to my work. With more than 5+ years experience as a professional, I have acquired the skills and knowledge necessary to make projects on a success. I enjoy every step of the understand requirement and project problems, design process, development flow, discussion, collaboration and delivery.
